Gazelle

It has a distinctive behaviour of stotting(running and jumping high before fleeing) when threatened by predators.

Scientific Name: Gazella
Average Length: 61-110cm
Average Lifespan: 10-12 years
Habitat: Grasslands and Savannas of Africa

A gazelle is any of many antelope species.It is a swift animal as it can run at a speed as high as 100km/h.Gazelles like to live in herds and eat less coarse,easily digestible plants and leaves.Gazelle predators not only includes humans but also cheetahs,lions,leopards,hyenas,wild dogs and pythons.
